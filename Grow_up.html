GROW UP

  The four little rascals are so thin.They are not yet strong to catch prey.I should to get some food to feed them.

  After eating a few rabbits.They have a little strength.They can follow me.

  But their curiosity is too strong. I always worry about losing them so I often call them.

I catch prey with my children.In order let my children to survive in the cruel nature,I must teach them everything I can.The food is not enough for my faimly.I always give the food to the children first.

We happily struggled through the three seasons.Until Winter.
